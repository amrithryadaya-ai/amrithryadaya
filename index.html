About

Dr. Shubham Kumar Sharma is a Interventional Cardiologist at Amrit-Hryadaya Heart & General Clinic, Jaipur. He holds a DM in Cardiology from AIIMS, Jodhpur, and completed his MD in General Medicine from R.N.T. Medical College, University of Rajasthan, Udaipur. He earned his MBBS from Grant Medical College & Sir J J Hospital, Mumbai.

With over 5 years of experience, Dr. Sharma specializes in both interventional cardiology (Angiography, Angioplasty, Device Closure, AICD/CRTD, Pacemaker) and non-invasive cardiology (ECG, Echocardiography, TMT, Ambulatory BP Monitoring, Holter). He is renowned for his patient-centric approach and commitment to embracing the latest advancements in cardiology to ensure the best outcomes for his patients.
